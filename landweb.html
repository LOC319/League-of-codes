LEAGUE OF CODES



INTRODUCTION



LEAGUE OF CODES is an educational game website that is created to entertain students studying computer programming. The game is intended to be a practice game that will test the knowledge of students in terms of computer programming. League of Codes offers a 2 Dimensional interface that brings nostalgia and pleasing sight to the users.



It includes mazes where the bugs to decode will be encountered. It also features story mode where it is interactive and helpful for the students.

LEVELS AND STAGES



1ST TOWER

GET READY! You'll start with the basics! This level is designed to help you build confidence as you tackle simple coding challenges like basic HTML and CSS tasks, along with straightforward debugging exercises.



It's the perfect way to warm up and get comfortable before diving into more complex problems. Let's get started!

2ND TOWER

BE WARE, things start to get a bit more challenging! In this stage, you'll face more complex coding tasks, including intermediate HTML and CSS concepts, along with trickier debugging and troubleshooting scenarios.



Get ready to sharpen your skills and take your problem-solving abilities to the next level!

3RD TOWER

AT LAST!your coding skills will be put to the ultimate test! In this stage, you'll encounter advanced programming challenges, intricate debugging puzzles, and complex HTML and CSS scenarios.



Precision, patience, and problem-solving are key here—are you ready to tackle the toughest tasks and prove your mastery?"

ABOUT LOC



A game website created by STIers from the Institute of STI College Cubao, under the Technical Vocational Livelihood (TVL) strand with a focus on Information Technology. This platform serves as an assessment tool designed to guide Junior High School students from APEC Schools, promoting and enhancing their programming skills. The game focuses on key areas such as debugging, troubleshooting, and basic HTML and CSS, supporting students in their TLE Life-labs subject.

VISION AND MISSION



By the development of League of Codes integrated into the modules of APEC, it could stand as a guide for students to learn and practice computer programming in a more fun and engaging way. Captivating platform that alters traditional educational approaches, fostering a stimulating and immersive learning experience for students. A platform to practice student’s critical thinking, problem- solving, and coding skills. As we delve deeper into this study, the researcher propose a solution to tackle the influence of the game website on APEC’s JHS students’ computer programming proficiency, proposing learning strategies to empower students in their academic journey.
